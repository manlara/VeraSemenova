About

My name is Vera Bogatyreva. I am a professional violinist with 7 years of teaching experience and alumni of Novosibirsk State Conservatory (Russia). I have played violin since I was six. As a musician I belong to worldwide known Novosibirsk School of Violinists.

The tradition of teaching violin in Novosibirsk goes back to such legendary teachers as Matvey Lieberman, Zakhar Brohn and Marina Kuzina. They brought up an array of outstanding violinists. Among them are Maksim Vengerov, Vadim Repin and many others.

Since graduating from Conservatory, I played in orchestras, quartets, trios, duos, various ensembles as well as solo. Prima Vera Studio is the creative lab where I teach, learn from and collaborate with young violinists.
